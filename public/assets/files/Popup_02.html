Avelumab triggers your immune system
Over time, cancer tumours can acquire properties that turn these
immune cells ‘off’, reducing the body’s ability to fight cancer.
Avelumab restores the ability of the body to fight cancer by turning
the immune cells, ‘soldiers’, back on.
Your immune system is what helps to defend your body against disease.
It is made up of immune cells, which attack foreign invaders, such as
infectious organisms and also cancer cells.
